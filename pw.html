About Physics Wallah

Physics Wallah (PW) is a major Indian edtech company that provides accessible and affordable coaching for competitive exams (like JEE and NEET), school curriculums, and skill development. Founded by educator Alakh Pandey and Prateek Maheshwari, it stands out for its low-cost hybrid model spanning online platforms, YouTube, and hundreds of physical centers across India.
